Q4 2025 BUSINESS PERFORMANCE REPORT
=====================================

Report Period: October 1 - December 31, 2025
Report Date: January 15, 2026
Prepared By: Finance Department

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
Q4 2025 showed strong performance across all regions with total revenue of $2.4M,
representing a 23% increase year-over-year. Electronics category led growth with
Widget Pro and Cloud Connect products exceeding targets.

KEY METRICS
-----------
- Total Revenue: $2,437,500
- Total Units Sold: 8,450
- Average Order Value: $288.46
- Customer Satisfaction: 4.2/5.0
- Return Rate: 2.1%

TOP PERFORMING PRODUCTS
-----------------------
1. Widget Pro - $890,250 revenue (2,975 units)
2. Cloud Connect - $674,955 revenue (4,500 units)
3. Smart Sensor - $404,955 revenue (4,500 units)

REGIONAL BREAKDOWN
------------------
- EMEA: $950,000 (39%)
- Americas: $825,000 (34%)
- APAC: $662,500 (27%)

CHALLENGES
----------
- Supply chain delays for Widget Pro causing 2-week backorders
- Increased competition in Smart Sensor category
- Currency fluctuations impacting EMEA margins

RECOMMENDATIONS
---------------
1. Increase Widget Pro inventory buffer from 150 to 300 units
2. Launch marketing campaign for Smart Sensor differentiation
3. Explore hedging strategies for EMEA transactions
4. Consider bulk discount program for Cloud Connect enterprise customers

FORECAST Q1 2026
----------------
Expected revenue range: $2.6M - $2.8M (+7-15% QoQ)
New product launch: Super Tool Premium Edition scheduled for March
